    How this works

    This interface uses a request flow. Your request is created first, then submitted, then reviewed and completed through the controlled trade process.

    step 1 connect your wallet on BNB Chain for CIGO market rail and / or Ethereum for COSIGO reference layer
    step 2 choose the asset you want to move from and the asset you want to move into
    step 3 enter the amount you want to convert
    step 4 click Get quote to preview the result
    step 5 click Request swap to create your request
    step 6 review the request card and confirm the route, amount, and status
    step 7 click Send request to send your request for review
    step 8 wait for review and completion, then reload later to check final status
    status guide:
    Not yet submitted = request is still on this device only.
    Pending review = request was sent and is waiting for approval.
    Action required = check the request for the next step.
    Completed = the request lifecycle is finished.

    verify before action

    Always verify the asset, route, and destination before submitting a request.
